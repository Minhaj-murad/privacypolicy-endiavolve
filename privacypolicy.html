Privacy policy

This privacy policy outlines the information we collect from our users, how it is used, and with whom it may be shared.
1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information about you when you register on our platform or when you use any of our services such as creating an account to participate in courses. This includes:


  • Name and contact details (such as email address)
  • Information associated with your profile such as language, time zone, etc.
  • Your activity on the platform (such as which classes have you taken, your ratings/reviews, etc.)
2. How We Use Your Information

The primary purpose of collecting your information is to provide you with a better experience in using our platform. We use the data we collect from you for the following purposes:


  • To improve our services and products
  • To send notifications regarding new courses, updates, promotions, etc.,
  • To identify users who are abusing our services or violating any of our terms and conditions
  • For research and analysis (such as understanding user behaviour)
3. With Whom We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information with third-party organizations such as service providers, business partners, affiliates, or analytics companies that help us operate and improve our platform. However, we will not sell or rent out your personal information to any third-party organization.

4. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard security practices such as encryption and firewalls to safeguard your data, however, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data due to the inherent nature of the internet.

5. Changes To This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is subject to change without prior notice in order to reflect any changes in our services or legal requirements. We recommend that you review this policy periodically for any updates or changes. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@endiavolve.com.
